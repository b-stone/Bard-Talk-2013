Concerning Scalars

We have observed the following:

  • Closure: r * A = A

  • Distributivity I: r * (A + B) = r * A + r * B

  • Distributivity II: (r + s) * A = r * A + s * B

  • Associativity: r * (s * A) = (r * s) * A

  • Identity: 1 * A = A

Note: This is the topic of Math 3** (Computational Commutative Algebra)
